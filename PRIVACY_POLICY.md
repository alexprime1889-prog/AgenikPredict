# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 15, 2026**
**Last Updated: March 15, 2026**

Manogrand Inc ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates AgenikPredict (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our Service.

By using the Service, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password. If you sign up through a third-party authentication provider (e.g., Google, GitHub), we receive your name and email address from that provider.

**Payment Information:** When you subscribe to a paid plan, our third-party payment processor collects your payment details (credit card number, billing address). We do not store complete payment card information on our servers. We receive and store a transaction ID, subscription status, and billing history.

**User Content:** Documents you upload to the Service (PDF, MD, TXT files) and simulation prompts you enter. See Section 3 for how we handle User Content.

**Communications:** When you contact us for support, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your communications.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data:** We collect information about how you use the Service, including pages visited, features used, simulation parameters, timestamps, and session duration.

**Device and Connection Data:** We collect your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type, and language preferences.

**Cookies and Similar Technologies:** We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We use analytics cookies to understand how the Service is used. See Section 7 for details.

### 1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age. We do not collect biometric data, government-issued identification numbers, health information, or precise geolocation data.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve the Service; process your subscriptions and payments; generate AI-powered simulations and reports based on your uploaded documents; communicate with you about your account, updates, and support requests; monitor usage patterns to improve performance and detect abuse; comply with legal obligations; and enforce our Terms of Use.

We do not use your personal information or User Content to train AI models. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

---

## 3. User Content and Uploaded Documents

Your uploaded documents are processed by our AI engine solely to provide the simulation and prediction service you requested. Documents are transmitted using TLS encryption in transit and stored with encryption at rest.

**Retention:** Uploaded documents and simulation results are retained for the duration of your active account. When you delete a simulation or your account, associated documents are permanently deleted within 30 days. Backups containing your data are purged within 90 days.

**Access:** Only automated systems process your documents during simulation. Our employees do not access your User Content unless you request support that requires it, we are required to do so by law, or it is necessary to investigate a violation of our Terms of Use.

**Third-Party Processing:** Your documents may be processed by third-party AI model providers (such as Anthropic via OpenRouter) and knowledge graph services (such as Zep Cloud) as necessary to provide the Service. These providers process data according to their respective privacy policies and data processing agreements.

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

**Service Providers:** We share data with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including cloud hosting, payment processing, AI model inference, and analytics. These providers are contractually obligated to use your data only for the purposes we specify.

**Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, safety, or the safety of others.

**Business Transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

**With Your Consent:** We may share your information with third parties when you have given us explicit consent to do so.

## 5. Your Rights

### 5.1 All Users

Regardless of your location, you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you; correct inaccurate personal information; delete your account and associated personal information; export your data in a portable format; and withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@agenikpredict.com. We will respond within 30 days.

### 5.2 European Economic Area (EEA) Residents — GDPR

If you are located in the EEA, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). The legal basis for our processing of your personal data is: contract performance (to provide the Service you requested), legitimate interest (to improve and secure the Service), consent (for non-essential cookies and marketing communications), and legal obligation (to comply with applicable laws).

You also have the right to restrict processing of your personal data; object to processing based on legitimate interest; lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority; and not be subject to automated decision-making with legal effects (our AI simulations are analytical tools, not automated decision-making systems as defined under GDPR Article 22).

For GDPR-related inquiries, contact our data protection contact at privacy@agenikpredict.com.

### 5.3 California Residents — CCPA

If you are a California resident, you have the right under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) to know what personal information we collect and how it is used; request deletion of your personal information; opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information); and not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.

To submit a CCPA request, email privacy@agenikpredict.com with the subject line "CCPA Request."

### 5.4 Israel Residents

If you are a resident of Israel, your personal data is protected under the Privacy Protection Law, 5741-1981, as amended (including Amendment 13). You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@agenikpredict.com.

## 7. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

**Essential Cookies:** Required for the Service to function (session management, authentication, language preferences). These cannot be disabled.

**Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand how users interact with the Service. We use privacy-focused analytics that do not track users across websites. You can opt out of analytics cookies through your browser settings or our cookie preferences panel.

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. We do not participate in cross-site tracking or targeted advertising networks.
