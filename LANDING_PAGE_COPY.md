## SECTION 11: FAQ

**Q: How accurate are the predictions?**
A: AgenikPredict simulates a range of possible outcomes based on your data. Think of it as a sophisticated scenario analysis tool rather than a crystal ball. The value is in seeing perspectives and dynamics you wouldn't have considered — not in predicting exact outcomes with certainty.

**Q: What types of documents can I upload?**
A: PDF, Markdown (.md), and plain text (.txt) files. We're adding support for more formats including Word documents, spreadsheets, and web URLs.

**Q: How is this different from asking ChatGPT?**
A: ChatGPT gives you one AI's opinion. AgenikPredict creates thousands of distinct AI agents — each with unique personality, memory, and social connections — and lets them interact on simulated platforms. You get emergent group dynamics, not a single perspective.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Your documents are processed for the simulation and are not used to train any AI models. Each simulation creates an isolated environment. Enterprise plans offer on-premise deployment for maximum security.

**Q: What languages are supported?**
A: The interface is available in English, Hebrew, Russian, Spanish, and German. Simulations can process documents in any language supported by the underlying LLM.

**Q: How long does a simulation take?**
A: A typical simulation with 100 agents and 10 rounds takes about 15-30 minutes. Larger simulations with thousands of agents may take longer. You can monitor progress in real time.
